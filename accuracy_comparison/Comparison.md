# 🧠 Model Comparison

## 🌳 Decision Tree
- Achieved strong performance on test data  
- Captures patterns and relationships automatically  
- Provides probability score (risk level)  
- May be sensitive to data quality  

---

## 👨‍⚕️ Expert System
- Based on medical rules and domain knowledge  
- Highly interpretable and transparent  
- Does not depend on training data  
- Limited flexibility (rules must be predefined)  

---

## ⚖️ Key Differences
- **Decision Tree** → Data-driven prediction  
- **Expert System** → Knowledge-driven reasoning  

---

## 🎯 Conclusion
- Decision Tree is more accurate for predicting unseen cases  
- Expert System is more reliable for explanation and trust  
- Combining both provides **better and safer decision support**

---

## 🚀 Final Insight
> The integration of machine learning and expert systems enhances both predictive performance and interpretability, making the system more suitable for real-world medical decision support.
